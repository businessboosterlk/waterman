About Waterman

Three decades of water engineering, in one team

Waterman Environmental Systems (Pvt) Ltd is a professional company specialising in tailor-made water and wastewater treatment. Since 1990 we have handled the full journey, technology, design, engineering, supply, installation and long-term service, for clients across Sri Lanka.

  • A 30-strong team of engineers, chemists and technicians across chemical, mechanical and electrical disciplines.
  • An in-house analytical laboratory driving correct system design and real-world troubleshooting.
  • Equipment from leading manufacturers in the USA, Germany, France, Japan and India.
  • Annual servicing and maintenance contracts for non-stop, continuous operation.
